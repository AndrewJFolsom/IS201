Meet the Artist

Hi! My name is Andrew Folsom. I am a young aspiring composer and have always had a passion for music. I focus on piano and choral composition, as well as classical piano performance. I am studying business at BYU, but I continue to write new music often and am open to collaboration with musicians and directors looking to perform new exciting music for their audiences!
